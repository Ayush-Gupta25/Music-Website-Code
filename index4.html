Entrepreneurs in the Music Industry

Jay-Z

Role: Rapper, Business Mogul

Jay-Z is a hip-hop icon and the founder of Roc Nation, a full-service entertainment company. He has also invested in streaming service Tidal, building an empire in music, fashion, and tech.

Dr. Dre

Role: Producer, Entrepreneur

Dr. Dre is a legendary producer and co-founder of Beats by Dre, which was sold to Apple. He has influenced hip-hop production and business with his ventures and partnerships.

Rihanna

Role: Singer, Founder of Fenty

Rihanna is a global pop star who expanded into the beauty and fashion industries with her Fenty brand, redefining inclusivity and style in the industry.

Kanye West

Role: Rapper, Fashion Designer

Kanye West is known for his music and Yeezy brand, which revolutionized streetwear and partnerships in the fashion industry, making him a prominent figure in business.
